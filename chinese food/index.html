Compared to people in the Tang Dynasty, The Song Dynasty used advanced cutting and seasoning techniques.

The Song Dynasty developed very complex cooking skills

These skills helped the local food culture to flourish.

Tea was very expensive at this time, and was considered a luxury.

Because of coming from the prairie to the capital city, Yuan people were good at riding.

With the help of a bow and arrow, they were expert hunters.

Their food had a Central Asian style, which led to more milk products.

The shipping industry was advanced at that time, for example, the famous Marco Polo came to China, in the Yuan dynasty.
